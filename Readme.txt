Purpose of System:
The users are able to gain and manage related information according to their role. These information includes student info, job info, job group info, country info, company info, internship info and education info.


Technology:
Front-end: Angularjs2, css, javascript, bootstrap
Back_end: J2ee, Jax-rs(Jersey), Maven, JDBC
Database: Mysql
